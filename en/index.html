About us

About StElli in brief

We focus exclusively on the Ragdoll breed. Our goal is to breed cats that are not only beautiful, but above all healthy and balanced.

🧬

Genetic tests

All breeding cats are tested for HCM, PKD, FeLV and FIV with negative results.

🏡

Home environment

Kittens grow up right in our home, not in cages — among people, children and everyday life.

🤝

Support after adoption

We are here for you even after the kitten is handed over — with advice on nutrition, care and behaviour.
