Our work

Education, health tests and socialization — the three pillars our whole breeding programme is built on.

01

Education

In 2025 I completed a cat genetics course at Campus Felinarium — a seven-week programme focused on genetics, health and responsible breeding.

02

Health tests

All our breeding cats are tested for HCM, PKD, FeLV and FIV. All results are negative.

03

Socialization

Kittens grow up in our home with children, used to being handled and to all everyday household life.

Why it matters

Healthy parents, balanced kittens

The investment in genetics and socialization shows in every kitten that leaves us for a new home.

HCM & PKD testy

Prevention of heart and kidney diseases in both parents before every litter.

FeLV & FIV testy

Ruling out viral diseases for the safety of the whole cattery and new owners.

Socialization with children

Kittens regularly meet children, household sounds and visitors — nothing surprises them.
