About us

A story of two names, one love of cats and the StElli cattery.

My name is Petra Vávrová and I breed Ragdoll cats. Our StElli cattery was born out of pure love for this wonderful breed.

We named it after our two little co-breeders — Stella and Ellena. To us, all our breeding cats are also our pets who live with us throughout the house.

I believe we are able to raise fully socialized kittens with a focus on health, appearance and temperament.
